About us

After following the Grateful Dead on tour for 20 years. We just missed much of the culture and experiences of being on the road. So we opened Grateful Cheese. Here you can enjoy the same quality grilled cheese you would enjoy in the parking lot of any Dead show. And for the 10 times the price! You're Welcome!

Our Menu

  1. The OG - This is our bread and butter. Well, technically it's cheese, butter and bread. You can never go wrong with a classic!
  2. The No-Dairy - For all our lactose intolerant friends. This one has no cheese! It's literally just two pieces of bread.
  3. The Works - Cheese, bacon, lettuce and tomato. A grilled cheesed BLT if you want to be fancy about it.
